Hi there, my name is Valeria Zaide and I'm a User Experience designer based in Tel-Aviv, Israel.

Fun fact. During the past 10 years I've lived in 5 totally different countries: Belarus, China, Israel, USA and Germany (gosh, I'm tired of moving!). So I know a thing or two about localization and cultural diversity. I love to create useful, accessible, and appealing products for everyday people and always strive to become a better designer. As a UXD I focus on exploring user's goals, needs & behaviors and translating the research insights into creative designs, as well as building perfectly logical and simple structure and architecture.

If you want to learn a bit more about my experience, you can view my CV.
